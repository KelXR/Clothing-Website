About M & T

M&T is a worldwide apparel brand based in Indonesia that has been inventing and experimenting with new fashion trends. lts branches can be found all over the world. M&T sells fashionable men's clothing that is up to date with the latest fashion trends.

History of M & T

Michael began his career as a designer at Anggatama Store in 1970, when he met Toyzen. Michael Varian founded the M&T company with Toyzen in 1983. Michael, who was also a designer, created clothing that reflected his sense of aesthetics, beauty, and luxury, which appealed to society's upper crust.
